About Our Project

AI-Driven Wildlife Monitoring and Poaching Prevention uses computer vision and deep learning to protect endangered species in real-time.

Our Vision

Our Vision

We envision a future where technology and conservation work hand-in-hand, providing instant alerts the moment wildlife faces danger.

The Problem

The Problem

Poaching is a $23 billion criminal industry. Manual surveillance cannot cover large areas effectively, and delayed responses often mean species are lost.

Our Solutions

Our Solutions

Our AI-powered system monitors wildlife 24/7 using advanced computer vision (YOLOv8) and triggers instant multi-channel alerts:

▶ SMS notifications to forest officers
▶ Email alerts with detection snapshots
▶ On-site audible alarms

How It Works

How It Works


▶ Continuous video monitoring from cameras in protected areas
▶ AI-powered object detection for animals, humans, and vehicles
▶ Proximity analysis to identify threats near wildlife
▶ Instant multi-channel alert activation
▶ Dashboard monitoring for response coordination
